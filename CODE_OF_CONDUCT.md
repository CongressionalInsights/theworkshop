# Code of Conduct

## Our Commitment

We are committed to a harassment-free experience for everyone.

## Our Standards

Examples of behavior that contributes to a positive environment:

- Using welcoming and inclusive language
- Respecting differing viewpoints and experiences
- Giving and accepting constructive feedback
- Focusing on what is best for the community
- Showing empathy toward others

## Enforcement Responsibilities

Project maintainers are responsible for clarifying standards of acceptable behavior and are expected to take appropriate and fair corrective action in response to unacceptable behavior.

## Scope

This Code of Conduct applies within all project spaces, and in public spaces when an individual is representing the project or its community.

## Enforcement

Report unacceptable behavior to the maintainers using the contact process in `SECURITY.md`.

All complaints will be reviewed and investigated promptly and fairly.
